Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Investment Calculator app (the “Application”) developed by Phan Nhat Dang (“Service Provider”) as a freemium service. This service is intended for use as-is.

Information Collection and Use

The Application primarily performs financial calculations locally on your device. Any investment parameters you enter (such as investment amount, interest rate, contribution schedule, or time horizon) are processed directly on your device.

No personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or contact details) is collected by the Application.

The Application does not upload your investment calculations or financial inputs to any server.

In some cases, the Application may collect limited non-personal technical data such as:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, operating system version)
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Crash reports

This information is collected anonymously and helps improve the stability and usability of the Application.

Use of Artificial Intelligence in Content Creation

Some illustrative images, icons, or language refinements used in the Application may be generated or assisted by artificial intelligence technologies during the content creation process.

AI tools are used solely to support illustration, design, or translation. They do not provide financial advice or automated investment recommendations.

The Application is designed as an educational financial calculator. It does not provide personalized financial advice.

Third-Party Services

The Application may use trusted third-party services to improve functionality and manage purchases:

  • Firebase – anonymous analytics and crash reporting
  • RevenueCat – handling in-app purchases and subscriptions

These services may collect limited technical information (such as device identifiers or purchase events), but they do not receive or store your financial calculation inputs.
